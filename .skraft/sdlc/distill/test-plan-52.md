## Walking Skeleton Strategy

**Selected strategy: A — Full InMemory**

Rationale: The `CheckEligibilityQueryHandler` performs a purely in-memory evaluation (no repository, no external service). It instantiates `Vehicle` and `Driver` Value Objects, invokes `EligibilityPolicy.Evaluate`, and returns an `EligibilityViewModel`. No persistence or external I/O is involved. Strategy A is both necessary and sufficient.
