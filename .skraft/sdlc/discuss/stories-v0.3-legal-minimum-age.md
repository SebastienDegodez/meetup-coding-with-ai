# Stories — v0.3-legal-minimum-age

## Milestone Summary

- **Theme:** Legal minimum age update for vehicle eligibility
- **Sprint goal:** Apply the new regulation raising the minimum driving age from 18 to 21 for cars and motorcycles, so that the eligibility domain correctly refuses applications from drivers aged 18–20.
- **Non-goals:** Changes to the electric scooter minimum age (remains 16); changes to the high-power motorcycle experience rule.
